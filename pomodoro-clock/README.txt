A Pomodoro Clock is a time management technique used to maximize productivity. By default, a pomodoro consists of 25 minutes of work time, followed by 5 
minutes of break time. This clock allows you to customize session time and break time, and lets you pause and reset the clock. Plays an alarm to let you 
know whenever a session time or break time is over.

HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript | jQuery | Bootstrap | SASS
